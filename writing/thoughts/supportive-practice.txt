There are lots of people creating work online. I think it's likely that the majority receive little to no attention or messages of support, unless they are lucky enough to create something that resonates enough to be shared widely, or they share their work with their friends.

I think it's important to leave comments or send an email of support if I appreciate something. It adds a bit of humanity to the internet, which could otherwise become a blur of pixels.
